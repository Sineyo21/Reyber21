FRUIT SALAD

Ingredients:

♥1 CAN FRUIT COCKTAIL (BIG), ABOUT 10 CUPS
♥1 CAN CONDENSED MILK
♥1 CUP ALL-PURPOSE CREAM
♥1/2 CUP EDEN ORIGINAL, CUBED

Cooking Instructions:

↓Step 1
Drain the Fruit Cocktail syrup. Leave in the strainer for at least 10 minutes.
↓Step 2
Stir together the condensed milk and all-purpose cream. Whip until blended well.
↓Step 3
Add in the fruit cocktail and Eden Original cheese cubes. Refrigerate and serve cold.
